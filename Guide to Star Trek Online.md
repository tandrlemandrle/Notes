# Tips

Try not to double up on boff powers, or use two different attack patterns. 

Google each boff power on stowiki, and if it puts other powers on cd, don't use those other powers.

Get all reps maxed out even if you don't need the gear. They will appear in your passives and you may need the traits, ship modules and respecs.

If you buy a cstore ship, fleet version will cost only 1 module.

Do try to join a fleet lvl 85. Thats top level and some gear requires top level.

Do all of R&D by doing 20h tasks. At level 15, they will give you nice traits and you'll be able to craft nice stuff. Level to 20 only if you're bored.

You can enable autofire on your hangar and your weps by right clicking on them, but do so in order which weapon recharges fastest is enabled first.

Google everything and look if it's per cycle or per hit, or for a time period.

Try to login for at least 15 mins a day, if nothing else, just to do events, preferably an hour to do your daily missions too.

Resists have diminishing returns. Meaning if you all cast attack pattern beta on target, use pen mod, disruptors or whatever to reduce targets resists, it will have none.

While you can beat hull resists, shield resists cannot be beaten. Any beating of shield resists will simply result with additional bleedthrough.

Shields are like a 2nd layer of resists against torps, using weapons and skills and powers that bypass or ignore shields will kill target faster.

All energy weapons have small base dmg. All torps and mines have big base dmg. So, mods on energy weapons should be [DMG] , and mods on torps and mines should be [CRTD].

Sometimes only diff between you doing 100k dps and someone doing 300k is their max hit so crits (even from pets) are very much important.

Crit chance I recommend you get from watchers. Buy them on exchange, 2 eng, 2 sci, and 2 tac, total 6 of them. You can seat only 5 tho.

Killing opponents engines on crit means if you crit, their defense value goes to minus, making them easy pickings.

Defense value will make all dmg received smaller. You can increase it with speed too. Move as fast as you can by triggering your fortified engines with aux to sif, your conn officer from phoenix, epte and evasive maneouvers.

The moment your 2nd energy weapon shoots, it's already spending less power, because first one depleted it. Omega torp + kinetic cutting beam 2pc set bonus, and fleet warp cores will make sure you have enough wpnpwr.

Tactical Team, Enginering Team, Science Team and Hazard Emitters will clear debuffs. Polarize Hull will make you break free from tractor beam.

Once you buy a ship, it may have a trait or 2 or a console. Fly that ship to unlock those traits, and you can use trait and most consoles on other ships.

You can safely discharge all ships from events or cstore, as you can reclaim them later. Lockbox, fleet, phoenix, lobi and dil ships you should drydock.

Do not try opening a single or a few boxes per day. You wont win biggest prizes that way. Make sure you open a 100 at a time by clicking like a maniac. Same goes for Phoenix.

Primary specialization Temporal has Continuity as last skill. It will save you from getting killed.

The moment you get a ton of fleet marks, get fleet unlocks you need from fleet holdings.

My recommendation for cptn skills are these 2: https://ibb.co/GFZthXf https://ibb.co/2ss1x7K

If you fly an escort, I recommend you focus on your guns. Use dual heavy cannons (agony), stack as much as possible procs on your guns and crits (nullifiers), use appropriate isomags, get emergency weapons cycle trait from arbiter

If you fly a science ship, I recommend you focus on high dmg consoles, buy unconventional systems trait, and keep casting control powers to reduce cd on those consoles. Pets would be of use and torps and mines.

If you fly a cruiser type, I recommend you focus on attracting fire. Get sarcophagus ship trait, slot your threatening stance in spam bar, use tilly 2pc for hp regen, use phased biomatter or inhibiting polaron beam arrays.

Use both mouse buttons pressed at same time to pilot your ship. 

If you use beam arrays, fly directly below or above targets and stay there.

If you use cannons, do a fast flyby. 

If you use torps, fly to target, reverse engines and shoot.

If you have low turn rate, try to keep engines at half speed.

Try to extend uptime and reduce cd of your attack upgrade, it is essential for high dps required for elite content. Optionally, use haste.

```
/unbind_all
```

and then 

```
/bind_load_file D:\Space.txt
```

(or the path where you saved the file).

# Ground keybinds

Copy the following in notepad and save as Ground.txt. Then while you are on ground in STO, type 

```
/unbind_all
```

and then 

```
/bind_load_file D:\Ground.txt
```

(or path where you saved the file). 
Assign your abilities to tray 2 and 3, make sure basic weapon fire is in default location (tray 1 slot 1) and keep pressing key 1 during combat.
